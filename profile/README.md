# Mechaxil

**Mechaxil** is a maker-focused, open-source hardware ecosystem built for hobbyists, students, engineers, and developers who love electronics, robotics, microcontrollers, and DIY projects. Our mission is to make learning, prototyping, and building accessible to everyone through high‑quality components, affordable kits, and clear documentation.

---

## 🚀 What We Do

Mechaxil provides:

* **Electronics & Robotics Kits** – Beginner‑friendly kits designed to teach essential skills.
* **Open‑Source Hardware Projects** – Fully documented designs, schematics, and code.
* **Maker Tools & Accessories** – Components, sensors, drivers, modules, 3D-printing supplies, and more.
* **Educational Content & Tutorials** – Clear guides, build instructions, and behind‑the‑scenes development.
* **Affordable Products** – Low-profit margins so young makers and students can start building.

---

## 🧰 Project Philosophy

Mechaxil follows an **open-core philosophy**:

* Core designs, libraries, docs, and kit instructions are open-source.
* Advanced versions, premium kits, and special extensions are optional add-ons.
* Everything is made to be hackable, modular, and easy to integrate with your own projects.

We want a world where anyone—regardless of budget or background—can learn engineering and build cool stuff.
